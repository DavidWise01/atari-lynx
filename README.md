ATARI LYNX (LYNX) — a UD0 EXEREÚNESIS teardown + spec edition. The Lynx was the first colour, backlit handheld — and the most powerful of its day — with custom chips that scaled and rotated sprites in hardware, years ahead of rivals. It was also a battery-devouring brick that the cheaper, frugal Game Boy buried commercially. Render-not-invent, two-layer (datasheet fact vs lore); no ROMs/BIOS/code; manufacturer cited not minted.
